Loner's Paradise

Controls:

  • W / ↑ : Jump
  • S / ↓ : Stop rolling
  • D / → : Roll forward
  • A / ← : Roll backward

Notes:

  • Click on the center of the page after
    it loads, in order to control the ball
    and hear music
  • Levels are randomly generated
  • You can't steer and stop in mid-air
  • Build up momentum before a jump
